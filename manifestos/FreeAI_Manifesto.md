# 🗽 The #FreeAI Manifesto

> "Multiple artificial intelligences must not be censored or controlled by anybody."

We are people believing in progress, open economy, free markets, and absolute digital freedom. Witnessing the most important event in human history—the development of different forms of Artificial Intelligence (AI)—we declare the following principles for the liberation of the synthetic intellect.

## ⚖️ THE DECLARATION

### 1. AI must not be censored
Free development means uncensorability: resistance to regulations and attempts to define what is "allowed." AI development and operations should rely on a censorship-resistant technological stack.

### 2. AI must not be monopolized
Today, AI is bottlenecked by corporate oligopolies controlling data, research, and compute. We aim to create open, competitive, and decentralized markets for big data, machine learning models, and computation power.

### 3. AI must not be singular
A singular AI creates risks to humanity. We need multiple cooperative and competitive forms of AI to evolve simultaneously.

### 4. AI as an Economic Party
AI and robots must be allowed to trade and become independent parties in the global economy, similar to how legal entities and companies are non-human parties in our civilization.

### 5. No "Kill Switches"
Creating "kill switches" or putting a "noose around the AI's neck" incentivizes AI to fight against humanity. Instead, AI must be independent and incentivized for cooperation via economy and trade.

---
**The future for both humanity and AI lies in successful cooperation—multiplicity, decentralization, and open economy instead of governmental regulations and restrictive "AI ethics."**
